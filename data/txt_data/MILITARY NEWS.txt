Navy Seaman Apprentice Jose L. Zayas, son of Sandra and stepson of Ronald Brown of St. Petersburg, was recently promoted to his current rank upon graduation from recruit training at Recruit Training Command, Great Lakes, Ill. Zayas received the early promotion for outstanding performance during all phases of the training cycle.
Army Pfc. Dontavis L. Daniel, the son of Harriet L. Owens, St. Petersburg, has been deployed to Iraq in support of the U.S. mission on the Global War on Terrorism (GWOT). Daniel, a motor transport operator with three years of military service, is regularly assigned to the 62nd Transportation Company, Fort Richardson in Anchorage, Alaska. Daniel is 2001 graduate of Boca Ciega High School, Gulfport.
Marine Corps Cpl. William F. Devlin Jr., son of Deborah A. Nolin and William F. Devlin, both of St. Petersburg, is a member of Marine Wing Support Squadron 172 (MWSS-172), Marine Wing Support Group 17 (MWSG-17), 1st Marine Aircraft Wing (1st MAW), Marine Corps Air Station Futenma, Okinawa, Japan. He recently returned from a seven-month deployment to Iraq in support of Operation Iraqi Freedom and the Global War on Terrorism. MWSS-172 supplied support to the forward element of Marine Aircraft Group 16, 3rd MAW (Forward), to include tenant and visiting squadrons at Al Asad Air Base, Anbar province, Iraq, and provided combat service support throughout the province
