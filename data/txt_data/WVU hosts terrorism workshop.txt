Mar. 1--WVU will host a Preparedness and Response to Agricultural Terrorism workshop March 13-15 at the Ramada Inn and Conference Center, in Morgantown.
The course is designed to promote response and recovery from agricultural terrorism from a community perspective.
The class is restricted to 45 people to accommodate group discussion and break-out groups for small in-class projects. The registration fee is $100 per member for the three-day course, which includes lunch and breaks.
Registration must be done by contacting Tom McConnell, WVU Extension specialist, Farm Management, at TRMcConnell@mail.wvu.edu or call him at 293-6131, Ext. 4237, or Becky Casteel at Becky.Cas teel@mail.wvu.edu or at 293-6131, Ext. 4231.
Or view and download a registration packet at www.wvu.edu/~agexten/index. html and look under calendars.
