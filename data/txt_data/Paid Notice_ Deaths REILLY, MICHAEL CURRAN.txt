 REILLY--Michael Curran of Sleepy Hollow, NY and Ramsey, NJ. Probationary Firefighter FDNY. Born April 3, 1981 in White Plains, NY; died August 27, 2006 in the line of duty with Engine company 75 in the Bronx, NY. 
Beloved son of Michael F. and Monica Reilly. Cherished brother of Erin and Kevin of Ramsey, NJ. Dear grandson of Helen Curran of South Bend, Indiana. Also survived by numerous Aunts, Uncles and Cousins in NY, Connecticut, Michigan, Missouri, Texas and Ireland. Sergeant, US Marine Corps Reserve; served in Iraq with Marine Wing Support Squadron 472 in the 3rd Marine Air Wing specializing in aircraft recovery, firefighing and pilot rescue. Recipient of Selected Marine Corps Reserve Medal, Global war on Terrorism Expeditionary Medal, Global War on Terrorism Medal, Sea Service Deployment Ribbon, National Defense Ribbon and Armed Forces Reserve Ribbon with M Device. Also served with the Ramsey Rescue Squad and Ramsey Volunteer Fire Department and the Stratford, Connecticut Fire Department. Friends and relatives are invited to call at the Van Emburgh - Sneider Funeral Home, 109 Darlington Ave, Ramsey, NJ on Wednesday and Thursday from 2-5 PM and 7-10 PM. A Funeral Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated at St Paul RC Church, Wyckoff Ave, Ramsey on Friday September 1, 2006 at 10:30 AM. Interment will follow at Maryrest Cemetery in Mahwah, NJ. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Thomas Elsasser Fund, 204 East 23rd Street, NY, NY 10010. Please send tributes, memories and condolences at www. vanemburghsneider.com
