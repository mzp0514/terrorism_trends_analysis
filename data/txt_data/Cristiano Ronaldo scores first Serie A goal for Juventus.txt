The 33-year-old had so far failed to find the net after the first three games of theSerie A season, but the Portuguese opened his account as Juventus hostedSassuolo on Sunday afternoon.
The Old Lady showed some good combination play in the early satges of the first half, although clear-cut chances were hard to come by in the opening 15 minutes.
Juve had to wait until the 38th minute for their first meaningful opening as Joao Cancelo's cross was almost deflected in bySassuolo's Pol Lirola only for Andrea Consigli to produced a superb save.
