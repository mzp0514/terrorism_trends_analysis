Notable Incidents
9/6/2011, 8:00 a.m. Online credit card fraud through the bookstore. The transaction failed the verifying process and activated the "red flags" system.
9/22/2011, 12:15 p.m. A terrorist threat and assault incident was reported on campus. Investigation of the incident is ongoing. Public Safety issued no comment.
9/25/2011 1:20 a.m. At 34 Spruce Place (across from MCTC). A police report revealed that a male, heavily intoxicated, was assaulted and robbed by two females. Any information available about this incident should be notified to MCTC Public Safety.
9/30/2011, 9:30 a.m. A report of a male selling illicit drugs was made to Public Safety. Minneapolis Police Department officers were dispatched. Suspect was apprehended. Investigation is ongoing and no comments were issued by Public Safety.
MCTC  is participating in a lockdown drill exercise on Tuesday, Oct. 11 at 1:45 p.m. and at 6:55 p.m. The drill exercises are estimated to last approximately 10 minutes.
The goal is to gain emergency preparedness in case of acts of terrorism,  active shooter (suspect whose activity may cause serious injury or death and has not been contained.), assaults, or other incidents of workplace violence.
