Army Sgt. Anthony Charles Deardorff, son of Dr. and Mrs. Charles Deardorff, Lancaster, has graduated from the Special Forces Qualification Course at Fort Bragg, N.C., and was awarded the Green Beret. Navy Seaman Nicholas J. Detweiler, son of Robert and stepson of Diane Detweiler, Ephrata, recently completed U.S. Navy basic training at Recruit Training Command, Great Lakes, Ill.
#731680.wrk#He is a 2003 graduate of Ephrata High School. Navy Seaman Apprentice Steve G. Axe III, son of Sarah L. Axe, Manheim, recently completed U.S. Navy basic training at Recruit Training command, Great Lakes, Ill. He is a 2003 graduate of Manheim Central High School. Army National Guard Pvt. Johnathan D. Miller, son of David H. and Elaine Y. Miller, Gordonville, has graduated from basic training at Fort Jackson, Columbia, S.C. He is a 2001 graduate of Pequea Valley High School. Army Sgt. 1st Class Stephen M. Geib, son of Barbara J. and Jay N. Geib, Mount Joy, has returned to Fort Drum, Watertown, N.Y., after being deployed to Iraq. He is a member of the 2nd Brigade Combat Team, 10th Mountain Division, (Light Infantry) at Fort Drum. All soldiers in the brigade were awarded the Global War on Terrorism Medal, the Global War on Terrorism Expeditionary Medal, and the National Defense Service Medal for their participation in Operation Iraqi Freedom. Geib, an infantry platoon sergeant, has served 13 years in the military. He is a 1992 graduate of Hempfield High School.
