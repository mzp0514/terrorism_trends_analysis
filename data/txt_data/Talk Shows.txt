SUNDAY
- "This Week" - Topics: Terrorism and the U.S. response, and the
Clinton investigation and the future of his presidency. Guests:
Secretary of State Madeleine Albright and Reps. Nancy Pelosi,
D-Calif., Jennifer Dunn, R-Wash., and Paul McHale, D-Pa. 11:30 a.m.,
ABC.
- "Meet the Press" - Topics: U.S. strikes against suspected
terrorists, and Monica Lewinsky and President Clinton's future.
Guests: Secretary of Defense William Cohen, author William Bennett,
Rep. John Conyers, D-Mich., Patricia Ireland of the National
Organization for Women, Democratic strategist James Carville and
Republican strategist Mary Matalin. 11 a.m., NBC.
- "Fox News Sunday" - Topics: Clinton-Lewinsky and terrorism. Guests:
House Majority Whip Tom DeLay, R-Texas; U.N. Ambassador and Energy
Secretary Bill Richardson, Reps. Porter Goss, R-Fla., and Anne
Northup, R-Ky.; Democratic strategist Susan Estrich and Anne Graham
Lotz of AnGel Ministries. 9 a.m., WVAH.
- "Late Edition" - Topics: The strikes on Sudan and Afghanistan and
the Clinton investigation. Guests: National security adviser Sandy
Berger, former Vice President Dan Quayle, Sens. Dan Coats, R-Ind., and
Joseph Biden Jr. D-Del.; Reps. Robert Wexler, D-Fla., and Bill
McCollum, R-Fla., and others. Noon, CNN.
