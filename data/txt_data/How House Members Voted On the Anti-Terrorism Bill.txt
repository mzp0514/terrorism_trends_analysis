Following is the 389-to 22 roll-call by which the House today passed a bill expanding anti-terrorism measures. Voting "yes" were 205 Republicans, 183 Democrats and 1 independent. Voting "no" were 18 Republicans and 4 Democrats. X denotes those not voting. There is 1 vacancy in the 435-member House.
