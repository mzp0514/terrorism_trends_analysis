Northwest Presbyterian
Vacation Church School. Come experience a "Rainforest Adventure." Open to children who will be age 4 by Sept. 1 through sixth-graders. Registration ends Friday. During the week of July 21-25, each day will begin at 9 a.m. and conclude at noon. Children will experience Bible stories through music, art, science, games, worship and drama. The cost is $20 a child, with a $40 maximum per family. The church is at 4300 Northside Drive, Atlanta. Registration is available online at www.nwpcatlanata.org, or call the office at 404-237-5539.
Mount Vernon Baptist
"Communicating Before a Crisis: A Community Presentation on Faith, Disaster, Terrorism and Preparedness." Hear speakers who are experts on severe storms, earthquakes and disasters, and area ministers and representatives from the FBI Joint Terrorism Task Force and Department of Homeland Security. Saturday. Free. Registration begins at 8 a.m.; speakers are scheduled until 3 p.m. The church is at 441 Martin Luther King Jr. Drive N.W., Atlanta. For more information, call 404-516-3436, or e-mail brjackson@ussif.org
Peachtree Road United Methodist
Bon voyage concert for the Chancel Choir. Come give the Chancel Choir a great send-off as they leave on their Music and Mission tour to Ireland. Sunday, 7 p.m. The concert includes music by Henry Purcell, Edgar Bainton and Richard Dirksen performed as it will be presented on tour. Admission is $10. All proceeds from the first two concerts in Ireland will go to charities sponsored by the Abbey Street Methodist Church in Dublin. The church is at 3180 Peachtree Road N.W., Atlanta. For more information, call 404-240-8212.
